Cook'D

Welcome to Cook’D! We know that life can become very busy. As a result, your favorite recipes may become forgotten. Our mission is to provide you with a free and easy way to transform the food in your pantry into your favorite meals. First, select the ingredients you currently have on hand. As you select, Cook’D will automatically display meals that you can cook with the selected ingredients. Change the selected ingredients at any time and the meal selection will update automatically! If you see a meal you like, click on it and the full recipe will display. Ingredients you may be missing are displayed in red text for your convenience. Additionally, we have provided the options to browse our full recipe selection or to browse by a filtered cook time, just click on the “Show All Recipes” button or the “Filter” button. Finally, if you need to close the app to take a call, don’t worry! Our local storage feature will save your selections for your convenience. When you’re ready to select new ingredients, simply click the “Clear All Ingredients” button. We hope that you enjoy using Cook’D! Happy cooking!
